Casting Director

In 2016, I embarked on my casting journey. My love for cinema dates back to my childhood, where I would watch everything from Indian daily soaps to world cinema. Originally hailing from Darwha, Yavatmal, a small town near Nagpur, I moved to Mumbai in 2012 for college with aspirations of being a part of the film industry.

Over the years, I have been involved in various aspects of the performing arts, including street plays, theater backstage, and dance shows. I even worked as an assistant director before finding my calling in casting.

Casting is a profession that chose me, and I am grateful for it. My motivation is driven by my love for good stories that resonate with audiences and leave a lasting impact. My mission is to contribute to the creation of compelling narratives by selecting actors who can bring out the best in their characters and enhance the overall storytelling experience.
